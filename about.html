BALDWIN HOLDINGS CORPORATION (BHC)

A one hundred percent Black owned South African investment and management holding company established on 3 December 2012.

BHC is a governance driven, investment focused entity that provides strategic oversight, governance leadership, and corporate stewardship to its subsidiaries and investment portfolio, contributing to South Africa’s transformation agenda and economic growth.

Through its subsidiaries, **Baldwin Technologies** and **Ralehumo Corporation**, and its **Investment Facilitation Division**, the corporation advances innovation, sustainability, and financial empowerment to create long term value for stakeholders.

Corporate Governance Framework

A comprehensive governance structure aligned with King IV, the Companies Act, and all relevant national policies.

Board of Directors

The Board provides strategic direction, policy development, and oversight of performance and compliance. It comprises executive and non-executive directors to ensure balanced governance and objective decision-making.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Setting strategic direction and performance targets
  • Ensuring legislative and regulatory compliance
  • Overseeing financial reporting and sustainable value creation
  • Promoting ethical leadership and stakeholder engagement

Board Committees

BHC has established specialised committees to strengthen oversight and governance across critical areas:

  • Risk and Audit Committee:
    Ensures financial transparency, internal control, risk management, and cybersecurity compliance.
  • Credit and Investment Committee:
    Oversees investment strategy, funding mobilisation, project finance, and credit risk management.
  • Procurement Committee:
    Ensures transparent sourcing and compliance with PFMA, PPPFA, B-BBEE requirements.
  • Ethics Committee:
    Promotes compliance with POPIA, anti-corruption regulations, and integrity management.
  • Recruitment and Human Capital Committee:
    Oversees recruitment, transformation, remuneration, and organisational capability.

Investment & Funding Services

BHC provides specialised capital sourcing and investment facilitation services, supporting sustainable growth, infrastructure development, and innovation-driven investment for both subsidiaries and external clients.

Investment Mandate and Services:

  • Structuring and sourcing funding for subsidiaries
  • Capital sourcing and investor relations
  • Project finance structuring for debt, equity, and blended finance
  • Mobilising resources to promote innovation and industrial development
  • Investment readiness and due diligence support
  • PPP facilitation with government and state owned entities

Strategic Investment Focus

  • ICT, digital infrastructure, and Artificial Intelligence enabled solutions
  • Construction, industrial manufacturing, and property development
  • Enterprise and supplier development initiatives
