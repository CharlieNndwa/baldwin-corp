About Baldwin Holdings Corporation

Baldwin Holdings Corporation (Pty) Limited (BHC) is a one hundred percent Black owned South African investment and management holding company established on 3 December 2012. The corporation provides strategic oversight, governance leadership, and corporate stewardship to its subsidiaries and investment portfolio.

Who We Are

BHC is a governance-driven, investment-focused entity that contributes to South Africa’s transformation agenda and economic growth. Through its subsidiaries, Baldwin Technologies and Ralehumo Corporation, as well as its Investment Facilitation Division, the corporation advances innovation, sustainability, and financial empowerment to create long-term value for stakeholders.

The corporation ensures that all subsidiaries operate within sound governance frameworks, adhere to South African legislation, and follow international standards of ethical conduct, sustainability, and accountability. Its governance philosophy is firmly rooted in the principles of the King IV Report, the Companies Act (No. 71 of 2008), and regulatory frameworks guiding ICT, industrial development, and corporate conduct.

Through strategic leadership and structured oversight, BHC promotes operational excellence, compliance, ethical leadership, and innovation across all business units while supporting the national imperative of inclusive economic development within the Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment (B-BBEE) framework.

Empowerment & Transformation

Baldwin Holdings Corporation, a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor, is deeply committed to advancing South Africa’s transformation agenda. We view empowerment not merely as a compliance exercise but as a core business driver for inclusive economic growth. Our strategy is focused on stimulating skills development, fostering enterprise growth, and championing socio-economic development across the communities we serve.

This commitment is realised through various tangible initiatives. These include targeted skills development and ICT training for youth, women, and people with disabilities, alongside robust internship and graduate programs within our subsidiaries, Baldwin Technologies and Ralehumo Corporation. Furthermore, we actively cultivate supplier development partnerships with local Small, Medium, and Micro Enterprises (SMMEs).

Beyond direct economic participation, we focus on long-term societal impact. Our community-focused projects are centred on enhancing community connectivity and digital literacy, ensuring that technology serves as an enabler for everyone. By investing in people and local enterprises, BHC is dedicated to creating lasting value and a brighter, more inclusive future for South Africa.

Our Social Impact

As a B-BBEE Level 1 Contributor, our commitment extends beyond commerce, driving meaningful transformation and inclusive economic participation.
