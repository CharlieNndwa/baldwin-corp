Group Structure

The corporate family tree linking our strategic investments in Technology and Industrial Solutions.

Strategic Vision: Diversified Excellence

Baldwin Holdings Corporation provides strategic direction and oversight to its subsidiaries. Our structure is designed for focused growth, with Baldwin Technologies driving the digital future and Ralehumo Corporation powering the industrial and physical infrastructure sectors. This dual focus ensures resilience and broad market access.
