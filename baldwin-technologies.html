Company Overview

Baldwin Technologies (Pty) Ltd, founded in 1999, is a 100% Black-Owned South African ICT company providing **end-to-end digital, networking, fibre, AI, and managed infrastructure solutions**. We integrate innovation, intelligence, and infrastructure to support organisations navigating the modern digital landscape.

Through advanced software engineering, intelligent networks, AI-enabled systems, and enterprise-grade managed services, Baldwin Technologies delivers future-ready digital transformation solutions to public and private-sector clients across South Africa.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in digital transformation, delivering intelligent, secure, and sustainable technology solutions.
